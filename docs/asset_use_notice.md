2. manuscript-ready figure/table assets (e.g., PDF/Excel deliverables).

## Licensing note
The repository-level `LICENSE` (MIT) is intended primarily for scripts, code, and supporting documentation.

Manuscript-ready figures, tables, and other publication-style assets may be subject to separate copyright, journal policies, and/or reuse conditions. Users should seek permission where appropriate and should not assume unrestricted reuse of manuscript figures/tables solely based on the repository-level software license.

## Recommendation
If reusing content from this repository:
- cite the repository and associated manuscript appropriately
- verify reuse permissions for figure/table assets
- follow journal/publisher policies where applicable
